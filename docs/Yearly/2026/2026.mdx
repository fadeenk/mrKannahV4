We spent a few days configuring and testing until we ran out of things to try. After many attempts and an actual mechanic looking at it, we found out that the issue was with the truck's brain version and needed an update from the manufacturer.

#### Harddrive recovery

My uncle had an external harddrive that stopped working. I tried to help him recover all his data from it. ### Random projects

#### Safety deposit box

We had a safety deposit box that my parents use. However since we moved it was far from us now. I helped them setup a new one and retrieve all their belongings. I also closed the old one and moved things to the new one.

#### Car entertainment system

My parents car had an old entertainment system that became very slow and unresponsive. I bought a new one and installed it for them. It worked perfectly except for some software that isnt compatible with the newer system. I found an alternative and it worked better than ever! Also with the new system, I got the steering wheel controls and the stock backup camera to work which was a big plus!

### House maintenance

#### Water filteration system

My reverse osmosis water filteration (drinking water) system was overdue for a filter change. I was going to replace the filters myself but they were proprietary filters that cost way too much. I ended up buying a new system that uses standard filters with an extra set of filters for the same price. The installation was straight forward and I was able to install it myself. I just hade to buy an adapter (coupling) to connect the new system to the existing plumbing and it worked like a charm.

#### Screendoor not opening or closing

It seems like the sliding door was no longer sliding. Somehow the door was not in the track properly. I put it back in the track but it still did not work. I then rebalanced the door by adjusting the screws on the rollers. It was a bit tricky but I was able to do it and it worked like a charm. 

#### Security system

I had my security system however it was linked to my account and when my tenants moved in, they didnt want it so I turned it off. Now, that my tenants are settled in, they wanted to use it. So I factory reset the system and set it up for them. However, they didnt have a wired internet connection so I had to use a wifi/wired network adapter to connect it to the network. It was a bit tricky once connected the system was back online except for one of the power adapters for the cameras was not working. I replaced it and it worked like a charm. 

#### Doorbell

My tenants tried to install a video doorbell but it didnt work. I took a look at it and found that the wiring was not correct. In the process they managed to fry the transformer. I bought a new transformer and installed it for them. That fixed it.

## Month by month highlights

### January

The year was off to a rough start! I continued playing soccer but since it was the new year, we had a lot of new people. Some of those new people were extra rough when playing and I ended up injuring my toe 🥲 Which was bad considering I had an Asia trip coming up and I was supposed to do a lot of walking! Since then I stopped playing soccer and I'm focused on recovering as quickly as possible!

Then things started to get better, my friends visited and got to do a trial run of walking like crazy in prepartion for our Asia trip. After they left, my pther friend invited me to enjoy Ka circus show. Then Another friend visited and went for all you can eat Sushi! Then I enjoyed some great Korean soup with another friend. 

After that it was time for the first San Diego trip. I attempted to help and fix my friend truck, but that was not meant to be. Of course, it will not be a trip to SD if we didn't enjoy some tacos! Once we were done with the parties that we had to attend we were back home.

However, that didnt last long because I went back to San Diego a week later to take my friend and go to Los Angeles to fly to Asia! This was a long awaited and overdue trip, I was supposed to go with my friend back in 2019 but things kept getting in the way. Although, we had a blast on the trip, it was short and rushed a bit because we squeezed so much into it. Our first stop was Tokyo, I loved every second of it! However, we underestimated the cold, we had to do some extra shopping there to survive the cold. The food was amazing, the people and culture was extremely nice, the city was beautiful and loved the system, transportation everthing! I can't wait to go back again and enjoy it properly. Then we headed to Bangkok, Thailand. 

### February

Thailand was interesting and fun in a different way! My faviorate part was all the night and street markets everywhere open late. Just walking around and enjoying the chaos! I didnt even drink water at all, because we could very easily get fresh smoothies and juices everywhere! The food was delicious and cheap! We tried things until we could not longer eat. I tried every type of noodle I could find, grilled alegator and some very delicious and spicy bugs! Additionally, the cheap feet massages saved us from all the walking we did. Also, the city was full of fun and interesting instagramable tourist traps.

After that, it was time for our final destination, the Philippines! We only did Manila and didnt do any of the islands, mainly because we didnt have much time left. Instead, we decided to relax and enjoy the capital city. Thier chinatown was crazy and an unforgetable experince. Also, they had some very delicious street food. The best part was we could actually talk to some of the locals since they spoke english so we could make some friends and get some locals recommendations.

Once I was back in the states, I was planning on staying a few days in San Diego to recover from the trip. However, my friend was hospitalized so I ended up heading right back home. Luckily, my mom recovered and everything was good. We celebrated by attending the church's Valentine's day party. We also went to the Ethel M chocolate factory cactus garden for thier Valentine's day decorations. 

That was just the start of the celebrations and travel for the year! I celebrated the Chineses New Year twice, once with my family in Koreatown, and another time with my friend at UNLV. To wrap up the month, I went on a trip to LA with another friend, which was ton of fun! We went to the Griffith Observatory, Santa Monica Pier which I surprisingly have not been to before. 

#### March

After all that traveling, I continued to have trips however, they more more local! Me and my family, went to Lake Mead to enjoy the nice weather and go fishing, kayaking and spend some quality family time. Then after that, we did a trip to Primm for a car show, that we missed or didn't happend, and for the new [Sexxy show](https://www.instagram.com/sexxyshowlv) which I got free tickets to! Also March is the month when I get to enjoy the ASD Market week convention and get to do a bit of shopping. However, this time the companies didnt want to sell me things because of the Iran war and how it is affecting shipping so I only got to eat some of the samples.

Before the month ended, my firend and I went for Mexican street food and got an insanse streen corn meal and hot dog! It was very delicious and unhealthy! After that, it was time for the Iraq vs Bolivia world cup qualification soccer game. My dad, sisters' husbands and I went to go watch the game at the church hall. It was a great atmosphere and we had a great time!


#### April

Of course, sticking with this year's theme, my family and I went to San Diego to celebarte Easter and do our taxes. Since this was a family trip to San Diego, we had to do lots of shopping, see relatives and visit the beach. It was a fun experience we also got to enjoy some delicious food and spend quality time together. I also managed to squeeze in a catch up with more of my friends and enjoy a buffet or two.

The rest of the month was quieter, since I was preparing and planning our big upcoming trip to east coast! I also squeezed in a few fun projects, and another trip to San Diego for a graduation event, haircut and a bit of shopping before the big trip.
