Scheduling

We take care of you even before your scheduled consultation or treatment:

  • Transportation from your method of arrival, such as airport pickup, to your chosen accommodations.
  • Check-in assistance.
  • Transport to/from appointments, testing, treatment and all other medical needs.
  • Provisions for communication, such as internet connection, contact phone numbers and email addresses, and working local cell phone.
  • Assistance with admission for healthcare facility, including the registration process and in-person guidance whenever requested.
  • Translation service.
  • Check-in during all periods of recuperation, including private nurses whenever required to help with bathing, prescriptions, and rehabilitation.
  • For laundry and/or meal needs, room service is provided.
  • Assistance with all documents related to post-operative and recuperative care, such as discharge paperwork, prescriptions, receipts, and so on.
  • Arrangements for accommodations during recovery period for you and your family, including sightseeing, rental vehicles, restaurants, entertainment, and special needs.
  • Review of area cultural and shopping amenities, including resort information where applicable.
  • Transportation for method of departure and assistance upon arrival there, whenever necessary, including checking in luggage.
