Policies

Patient-First policy

.

In order to serve your individual needs to the best of our abilities, we create custom travel and treatment packages for you. We strive to make use of the most innovative medical technologies and the best travel accommodations available to everyone, regardless of their medical condition. You are suffering enough dealing with a medical problem, whether it is your own health or the health of someone you love, so we work hard to make sure that all of your travel issues are dealt with efficiently and effectively, so that you can relax and regain your health on your trip.

Our professional team of consultants custom design each medical tourism package, including those related to general medicine, pediatrics, dental procedures, or vision needs, connecting you with the most renowned healthcare providers and locations in every field of practice. It is our goal to set up a consultation between patients and leading health care providers presenting the most modern healthcare tools and resources to help you through your difficult situation.

Offers and Costs

You, the patient, are the center of our attention, whether you plan your health stay privately through CHES or through a government institution.

Cooperation with government institutions / embassies or private funding is also available.

As soon as we receive the inquiry and the medical reports from your embassy we contact different clinics to locate the most appropriate specialist available the optimal therapy for you.

Within 48 hours you will receive comprehensive answers to your questions: What does the expected schedule look like? Which costs will arise? How fast can you be treated? We deal with the necessary formalities directly with your embassy and include these in the treatment process where necessary.

Get picked up by us directly from the airport and be brought, along with your luggage and your family, to your hospital or your hotel. We are of course at your disposal on-site with our patient offices, and also take care of your return transport to the airport at the end of your stay.

If your embassy involvement is necessary in taking on the costs for both the medical facilities and for the recommended treatments. The costs for additionally desired services are borne directly by the patient just like for hotel or apartment reservations. We offer you here exclusive comfort as well.

Before your arrival in United States Of America, 65% of the cost estimation must be paid in advance. The remaining balance must be paid within 48 hours of arrival. All other services not mentioned in contract agreement will be given separate billing invoices to be paid within 48 hours to ensure proper schedule arrangements. It is highly recommended for patients and or their responsible party to take care of ALL financial liability arrangements in advance to concentrate of patient recovery and comfort. Do you have any questions? Please contact us.
