Partners

.

CHES has located the best doctors in California for every specialty, so that we can make them available to CHES's patients from other parts of the world. We also connect doctors with each other, so that they can pass along their knowledge and expertise between each other and maximize their potential as physicians.

Our relationships with a variety of government agencies and the leading healthcare providers help us to pass along a wealth of knowledge from all over the world. This includes helping physicians learn of new study opportunities, from continuing education to innovative techniques or medications.
